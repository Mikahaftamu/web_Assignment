About Enterprise Rent-A-Car

Enterprise Rent-A-Car is an ongoing American success story. Our guiding principles and humble beginning revolve around personal honesty and integrity. We believe in strengthening our communities one neighborhood at a time, serving our customers as if they were our family, and rewarding hard work. These values remain true today, just as they were when we were founded in 1957.

Today, Enterprise is the largest transportation solutions provider, offering car and truck rentals, car sharing, and car sales. With over 8,000 locations worldwide, we’re there when you need us. We actively participate in sustainability efforts, not only because it’s smart for our business, but because we believe in making the world a better place for future generations.

Did you know? Founder Jack Taylor selected the name “Enterprise” as a salute to the WWII aircraft carrier he served on, the USS Enterprise. Today, the “Enterprise” name is synonymous with leadership and vision in the business.

Milestones

  • 1957: Jack Taylor founds Executive Leasing Company in St. Louis with seven cars and a hunch that customers will embrace the novel concept of leasing automobiles.
  • 1962: Executive Leasing Co. adds a rental car business division with a fleet of 17 vehicles, catering to customers who need to rent cars while theirs are in the shop.
  • 1969: Jack renames his growing operation “Enterprise” in honor of the aircraft carrier aboard which he served in World War II, marking the company’s expansion beyond St. Louis.
